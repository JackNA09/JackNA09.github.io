Play My Game

Click here to play.

I spent a lot of time trying to make many unique and fun encounters and scenarios. Each of the nine maps has its own specially created events and detailed enviroments. This game has a lot of fun and challenging boss battles that give you a reason to explore and find new, and valuable items. There is a freedom of choice approach to the game, so you can shape your characters reputation and how he goes through the game. You can find new allies across the different maps, and if you interact with them correctly, they will join your party.
